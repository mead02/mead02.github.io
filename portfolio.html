4chan Research Project

/pol/ and Donald Trump: From doubt to love

University of Minnesota Twin Cities

My Research Project was for WRIT 3577W. This paper goes over how 4chan's /pol/ board went from not being interested in Donald Trump to actively supporting his run for presidency in 2016. The report has many screenshots of posts and external literature reviews of other works covering /pol/ and Donald Trump. The change over public opinion over time is illustrated with screenshots and key quotes, and conclusions and recommendations for future research are also included. The uniqueness of 4chan in our current net landscape is also discussed in relation to how the users' make political decisions.

  • Synthsizing information
  • Editing graphics
  • Creating and using a stylesheet
  • Advanced document formatting in Word
  • Presenting an unbiased view

COVID-19's Effect on Grocery Stores Formal Report

The COVID-19 Pandemic and the
Largechain Grocery Industry:
Impacts and Changes

University of Minnesota Twin Cities

My formal report was made for WRIT 3562W. The report goes over how the grocery industry was impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic. There is some review of current sources covering the changes as background, but the main focus is on interviews I did with multiple workers in a large-chain grocery store and their experiences. I created some infographics to match the data, and there is also an analysis of the findings and recommendations for future research.

Skills Used

  • Conducting original research
  • Interviewing people of different backgrounds
  • Creating graphics in Photoshop
  • Synthsizing information
  • Advanced document formatting in Word

LOVENOX Quick Injection Guide

LOVENOX Quick Injection Guide
Doctor's Office Poster

University of Minnesota Twin Cities

This poster was made to give a quick overview of how to administer a dose of LOVENOX medication. Since the medication is only available as a self-administered injection, many patients will need to learn how to properly use the product. This poster was made fully in Adobe Photoshop, and all photos were taken by myself. Due to limitations in partipants, an orange was used instead of a patient to demonstrate the injection process. The design of the document was chosen to appeal to all ages, as some younger patients may be prescribed LOVENOX.
